Who am I?

Edward Ho Jun Lim. Korean American. Born on August 5th, 1995 and raised in Koreatown, Los Angeles, California. 21 years old. Leo.

I graduated from University of California, San Diego with a bachelor's degree in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction. I am an aspiring User Experience/User Interface/User Research developer. I got into UX/UI and web design because I wanted to apply my creative side into a skill that really uses my full potential. Countless times, I struggle with navigating websites and for me to be a part of the kind that fixes usability errors, means that I can deliver intuitive work that comforts people.

Well, I'll start with college. College has been an adventure. I did things I regret, and things that I don't. I went down paths I didn't even think of when I was in high school. I joined the Sigma Alpha Epsilon Fraternity. I'm starting to play a lot more golf. I'm starting to drink a lot more, going out more. Just living my life a lot more. It's definitely been an experience where I try to find myself and who I am and who I want to become. Do I want to be the type of guy who sits in the office all day? Maybe. Do I want to be the guy who does a lot of field work? Maybe. Do I want to be a military man? Maybe.

But one thing that I know for sure is that I want to travel. Explore the seven seas and conquer my small fears that I have accumulated over the years. Experiencing the world first-hand, in my opinion, is the best way to learn and grow up to be more culturally aware, which is definitely something all of us need a litle more in this time and age.
